# Product Overview

> **Status**: Name, core concept, and branding confirmed.

---

## Product Name
**Future**

---

## What It Is
Future is a browser-based creative experience. The user uploads a video or audio
recording of themselves playing a song. The app extracts the audio, analyzes it in
real time (beat detection, BPM, frequency spectrum), and generates a reactive 3D
skateboarding animation:

- A **procedurally generated landscape** whose terrain height, color, and obstacles
  morph in sync with the music's beat and frequency bands (powered by Three.js).
- A **custom skater character** whose physics — speed, jump height, gravity, trick
  execution — are driven live by the incoming audio signal (Web Audio API).

---

## Core Value Proposition
Transform any music recording into a living, shareable 3D skate world.
Your sound is the controller.

---

## Users
- **Primary**: Future-forward musicians and creators who want to see their sound
  visualized as a living, physical 3D world.
- **Secondary**: Creatives and hobbyists drawn to generative 3D art and
  music visualization.

---

## Key Features

| # | Feature | Description |
|---|---------|-------------|
| 1 | **Video / Audio Upload** | Upload a video of yourself playing or any audio file; the app extracts and processes the sound. |
| 2 | **Real-Time Audio Analysis** | Web Audio API beat detection, BPM tracking, and frequency spectrum analysis. |
| 3 | **Reactive 3D Terrain** | Three.js procedural landscape that deforms, recolors, and populates with obstacles in response to music. |
| 4 | **Physics-Driven Skater** | Custom skater character whose speed, jump force, gravity, and tricks are governed live by the audio signal. |
| 5 | **AXON — Original Custom Character** | Fully original low-poly geometric skater (name: AXON) — no third-party IP. Entirely owned by the product. |
| 6 | **Share / Export** | Share a link or export the generated animated skate world. |
| 7 | **Broad Format Support** | Accepts MP3, WAV, OGG, MP4, MOV, WebM — up to 200 MB. Audio extracted automatically from video formats. |

## Tech Stack
- **Three.js** — 3D rendering, procedural terrain generation, physics simulation
- **Web Audio API** — audio analysis, beat detection, frequency response
- File upload pipeline for video and audio formats

Crazy. Bold, wild, expressive — the north star feeling confirmed by the creator.
The energy of a music video crossed with a skate film. Dynamic and designed to be shareable.

**Visual brand** (confirmed — Designer pass complete):
- Palette: electric purple / violet, cyan, electric blue on dark backgrounds
- Logo: neon waveforms + 3D terrain energy + skater silhouette, "FUTURE" wordmark

---

## Positioning
Not a DAW. Not a generic music player or visualizer.
A personal music visualization toy: upload your music, receive a living 3D world.

---

## Scope (MVP)
1. Upload a music video or audio file
2. 3D skate world visually and physically reacts to the beat and frequencies
3. Skater physics governed entirely by the audio signal
4. Shareable link or export for the generated animation

---

## Strategic Principles
- The audio signal is the only "controller" — no manual scene adjustments required
- The character is original and fully owned by the product
- Zero technical skill required from the user: upload and watch
- Every song produces a unique world (fully generative, never the same twice)
